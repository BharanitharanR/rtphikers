Our Mission:

At RTP Hikers Guild, our mission is simple yet profound: to inspire and empower novice hikers to embrace the wonders of hiking while fostering a sense of camaraderie and respect for nature. We believe that hiking isn't just about conquering trails; it's a transformative experience that connects you with nature, improves physical fitness, and introduces you to a welcoming community of outdoor enthusiasts.

Why Choose RTP Hikers Guild:

  • Novice-Friendly: We are novice hikers who are just starting their hiking journey.
