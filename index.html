Our Mission

At RTP Hikers Guild, our mission is to inspire and empower novice hikers to embrace the wonders of hiking while fostering camaraderie and respect for nature. Hiking isn't just about conquering trails; it's a transformative experience that connects you with nature, improves fitness, and introduces you to a welcoming community of outdoor enthusiasts.

Why Choose RTP Hikers Guild

We're dedicated to providing a novice-friendly environment for hikers just starting their journey. Here's what sets us apart:

  • Guided hikes tailored for novice hikers
  • Local expertise in RTP's scenic trails
  • Emphasis on safety and hiking essentials
  • Community and lasting friendships
